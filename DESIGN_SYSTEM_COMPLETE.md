1. **Consistency**: Gold accents used consistently for all interactive elements

3. **Premium Feel**: Sophisticated gradients and smooth animations
4. **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios and clear typography
5. **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first approach with comprehensive breakpoints
